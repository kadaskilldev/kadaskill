Welcome to KadaSkill

A Microlearning Platform Empowering Diskartech Employees with Bite-Size, High-Impact Learning in Cloud, AI, and Cybersecurity

Built by Diskartech Solutions Inc., KadaSkill fuses the Filipino "barkada" spirit with microlearning best practices to deliver bite-sized cloud, AI, and cybersecurity courses that keep consultants competitive and future-ready anytime, anywhere.

MISSION

Empowering businesses through innovative, secure, and intelligent technology solutions while nurturing continuous learning and excellence within our people and partners.

VISION

To become the Philippines' leading provider of future-proof cloud, AI, and cybersecurity consulting services — recognized for our excellence, agility, and Filipino ingenuity.
